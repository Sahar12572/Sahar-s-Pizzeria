Sahar's Pizzeria & Pasta House

Our Story

At Sahar's Pizzeria & Pasta House, our journey started with a simple love for great food and family traditions. Inspired by generations of homemade recipes, we set out to create a place where everyone feels at home. From our very first pizza to the rich, comforting pasta dishes we serve today, our passion for quality and flavor has never changed. Every meal we make is a tribute to the warmth of family gatherings, laughter around the table, and the joy of sharing delicious food.

Our Food


We believe that great food starts with great ingredients. Our hand-stretched pizzas are made with the finest dough, slow-risen for the perfect crisp and chew. We top them with rich tomato sauce, creamy mozzarella, and the freshest toppings. Our pasta is made with love, featuring house-made sauces simmered to perfection. Whether you’re craving a classic Margherita pizza, a hearty plate of fettuccine Alfredo, or a bold, spicy arrabbiata, every dish is crafted to deliver a taste of true Italian comfort.

Our People

Our team is more than just staff—we’re family. From our passionate chefs who craft every dish with care to our friendly servers who treat every guest like an old friend, our people are at the heart of everything we do. We believe in creating a warm, welcoming atmosphere where everyone, from our team to our guests, feels at home. With a shared love for great food and genuine hospitality, The Waheeds' is not just a restaurant—it’s a place built by people who truly care.
